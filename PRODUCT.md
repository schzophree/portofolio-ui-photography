# PRODUCT: Moch Danu Hidayat Portfolio

## Users
- Potential clients (couples, event organizers, brands) looking for high-end photography and videography.
- Art directors and creative agencies looking for visual collaborators.
- Photography enthusiasts and community members in Pontianak.

## Brand: Cinegraph.id
- **Identity**: Professional, artistic, and cinematic.
- **Voice**: Authoritative yet inviting, focusing on storytelling and visual impact.
- **Register**: brand

## Tone
- **Cinematic**: Every shot feels like a still from a high-budget film.
- **Luxury**: Dark, moody, and premium.
- **Elegant**: Refined typography and sophisticated layouts.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS**: Avoid bright blue accents, flat illustrations, and standard card grids.
- **Clutter**: Avoid overwhelming the user with too much text; let the visuals speak.
- **Cheap Effects**: No cheesy animations or over-saturated colors.

## Strategic Principles
- **Visuals First**: The photography is the hero. Use full-bleed images and generous whitespace.
- **Editorial Feel**: Treat the portfolio like a high-end fashion magazine.
- **Smooth Interaction**: Use motion to guide the eye and create a premium feel, but keep it subtle.
